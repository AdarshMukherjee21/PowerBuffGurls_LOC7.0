Tax Benefit Details

Section 80G Benefits

Under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, companies can claim up to 50% deduction on qualifying CSR contributions.

Qualifying Causes

  • Education
  • Healthcare
  • Environment
  • Poverty Alleviation
  • Rural Development

Important Notes

  • Contributions must be made to registered organizations
  • Proper documentation is required
  • Benefits are subject to annual limits
